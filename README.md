## üì° The Art of Interstellar Communication

In GalacticCom, words have weight and travel physically through space. Communication is an art divided into two distinct experiences.

### Casting a Transmission into the Void

On the **Transmit** screen, you can share your thoughts with the cosmos. But there are rules:

*   **Limited Broadcasts:** Resources are scarce in deep space. You can only send a limited number of public transmissions per day. Choose your words wisely.
*   **A Message:** Your message is launched into the void and travels to a **random galaxy**, where a lucky inhabitant may receive it. You never know who will hear your voice.
*   **The Test of Patience:** Your words take time to cross the universe. The arrival time depends on your experience as a communicator.

### Tuning into the Echoes

On the **Broadcast** screen, you don't speak; you listen. Tune your consciousness to capture the transmissions of other travelers echoing in **your current galaxy**.

*   **Thought Balloons:** Messages appear as ephemeral balloons of energy. Catch them before they fade away.
*   **Intercept & Connect:** If a message resonates with you, "intercept" it to open a private, secure channel with the sender.

### Secure & Private Comms

When you connect with another traveler, a private link is established.

*   **End-to-End Encrypted:** Your private chats are secured with military-grade encryption. Only you and the other person can read them. Keys are stored only on your device.
*   **Rich Media:** Share images, GIFs, and short voice transmissions to bridge the distance.
*   **Symmetrical Deletion:** Privacy is paramount. If one person deletes the conversation, it vanishes for **both**, severing the link completely.

## ‚ö° The Chaos of the Cosmos

The universe is not a safe place. Your transmissions are at the mercy of cosmic events that add a layer of strategy and unpredictability:

*   **Solar Storms:** Can **corrupt** parts of your message, altering its meaning.
*   **Gravitational Anomalies:** Can **delay** your transmission, drastically increasing travel time.

You will have to be patient and adapt. Your ability to communicate despite the challenges will prove your worth.
